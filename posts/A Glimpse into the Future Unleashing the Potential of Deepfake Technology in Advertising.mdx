## The  Rise of Deepfakes: A Technological Marvel

Before we delve  into the realm of deepfake advertising, let's take a moment to understand what deepfake technology is all about. Deepfakes are synthetic media that use artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms to manipulate or generate visual and audio content  that appears  strikingly authentic. By training on vast datasets, deepfake algorithms can analyze and mimic the facial expressions, movements,  and voices of real individuals, seamlessly blending them into fabricated scenarios.
